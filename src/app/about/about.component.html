About me

Hello!

My name is Kassidy Heath, and I am the first employee and head brewer behind Sunuva Booch.
I fell in love with Shrewsbury (and one of its lifelong residents) in 2015. Last Summer I finally got the guts to move across the pond to join my new family and this wonderful community.

First foray

I first tried Kombucha in college in Boston. It was nice, but it wasn’t quite ‘love at first sip’. It took me until lockdown, when I had gained weight and developed a drinking problem (yikes), to start looking for healthier alternatives to soda, alcopops and beer. And BOOM – there was Kombucha. Sweet, sweet Kombucha.

Flavours!

It was suddenly the most delicious beverage on planet Earth. I started buying it by the gallon, trying all the brands and all the different flavors I could find. I was drinking significantly less alcohol and calories. I felt energized, athletic, and alive!

Eco-friendly

When I moved to the UK, I was surprised that I couldn’t find Kombucha as easily. It just hasn’t taken off here yet like it has in other parts of the world. The Kombucha I could find in stores was disappointing as it didn’t have the same freshness, nutrient content, or flavor, and had usually been shipped from far away or packaged in plastic – not so eco-friendly.

Brewing

So I decided to put my Biology degree to use, and started brewing my own Kombucha at home. I did a lot of research, made a ton of mistakes, and spilled enough tea for a second Boston Tea Party – but the resulting brews came out fantastic! With meticulous process development and recipe refinement, I’m very confident in my product and am excited to introduce Kombucha at its best.

I can’t wait to hear what you think!
